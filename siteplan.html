Project Site Plan

Site Name

Example Site Name: Bay Area eBiking Club

This name represents a club focused on electric biking in the San Francisco Bay Area.

Optional Domain Availability: bay-ebike.org

Site Purpose

The site will provide a hub for regional e-biking by listing trail and e-bike recommendation information along with a membership information form.

Scenarios

  • What is the best e-bike for mountainous areas?
  • Where can I find contact information for the club's directors?

Navigation Structure

  • Home - Overview of the site
  • About Us - Information about the project
  • Services - Details about services offered
  • Contact - Contact form and details

Accessibility Considerations

The site will include accessibility features such as alt text for images and keyboard navigation support.

Content Management and Update Plan

Content will be managed manually, with updates made as necessary to keep information current.

Performance Optimization

Images will be optimized and CSS/JavaScript files minimized to ensure good performance.

SEO Considerations

Basic SEO strategies will be implemented, including meta tags and descriptions.

Error Handling and Validation

Form validation will be handled both client-side and server-side to ensure data integrity.

Backup and Recovery Plan

Regular backups will be taken, and a recovery plan will be in place to handle data loss.

Testing Plan

Usability, cross-browser, and mobile responsiveness testing will be conducted.

Legal and Privacy Considerations

A privacy policy and legal disclaimers will be included on the site.

Hosting and Domain Details

The site will be hosted on GitHub Pages, and a domain name will be registered if applicable.

Maintenance Plan

The site will be maintained with regular updates to content and software.
